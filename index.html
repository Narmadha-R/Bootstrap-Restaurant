    About Us

    TasteOfIndia processes and sends orders directly to partner restaurants, which then deliver to their customers. The service is available via its websites and mobile applications.Customers order food by entering their postcodes on the site and browsing for food from a list of restaurants. They can create meals by browsing restaurant menus and selecting items they want to order before entering an address and proceeding to the checkout.
